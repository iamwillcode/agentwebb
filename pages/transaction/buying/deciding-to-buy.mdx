# Are you considering buying a new home?

It's important to take a moment to reflect on why you're looking to buy and make sure your motives are sound. Ask yourself some key questions to separate any emotional conflicts from actual reasons that would make your decision to buy a smart and successful one.

![link](/media/deciding-to-buy.webp)


## Good Reasons to Buy

Examples of good reasons to buy include:

* A job relocation with a solid company and bright future
* A new job or promotion with a positive impact on both your life and your partner's life
* A growing family, with a new child on the way
* Careful consideration for a year or more, without impulsive decision-making

### Bad Reasons to Buy

Examples of Bad reasons to buy include:

* Feeling inadequate compared to friends who have nicer homes
* Being impressed by a house during a casual drive
* Hoping to resolve serious financial issues through buying a new home

#### It's important to evaluate your reasons! 

Be honest with yourself. By doing so, you can make a smart, informed decision that will lead to a successful and fulfilling home purchase.
